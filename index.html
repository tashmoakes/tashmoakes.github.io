About Me

Hayley Westlake is based in Portsmouth, UK and teaches wannabe bendies Awakening Yoga, a vigorous and fluid vinyasa yoga practice that builds strength, creates flexibility and inspires play. Hayley’s teaching goes beyond intelligent alignment; moving any willing student to an introspective, holistic experience.
